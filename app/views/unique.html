Background

Unique Studio, a pioneering student-run studio in HUST that focuses on Internet technologies, updates its official website annually to keep track of tendency of web design as well as update the studio's latest news. As the director of the Design Team of the studio at the time, I was responsible for the design for 2014-2015 version.
